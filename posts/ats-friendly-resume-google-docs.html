1. What Is an ATS and Why It Matters

An Applicant Tracking System (ATS) is a digital gatekeeper. It scans resumes for keywords, contact info, and structure, then filters out the rest. If your resume doesn‚Äôt follow a readable format, the ATS simply skips it.

That means:

  • Graphics, icons, and images = no-go zones
  • Tables and columns may not parse properly
  • Headings need to be standard (e.g. ‚ÄúWork Experience‚Äù)
  • Fonts must be common ‚Äî think Arial or Calibri

Your goal: make every detail of your resume readable ‚Äî both by machines and hiring managers.

3. Build a Solid ATS-Compatible Structure

Contact Information at the Top

Include your:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • LinkedIn URL (plain text)
  • City and state (or region)

Why not a header? ATS systems sometimes skip headers, missing vital info.

Use Standard, Hierarchical Headings

Never reinvent the labels. Stick to:

  • Professional Summary
  • Skills
  • Work Experience
  • Education
  • Certifications, Projects, or Awards (if relevant)

Use Google Docs built-in heading styles (Heading 2) ‚Äî this helps both ATS and readability.

Reverse Chronological is the Way

Order jobs from most recent to oldest. Use bold job titles, followed by company, location, and dates in MM/YYYY format. Add bullet points describing:

  1. Your role and impact
  2. Measurable achievements (use percentages, numbers)
  3. Relevant skills and tools used

Skills Section: Plain, But Powerful

Include a simple list of 8‚Äì12 skills, separated by commas or bullets. Cover both hard and soft skills. No graphics or ratings.

FAQs on ATS‚ÄëFriendly Resumes in Google Docs

Is Google Docs good for ATS‚Äëcompatible resumes?

Absolutely ‚Äî as long as you stick to clean text formatting and avoid tables, images, and headers.

Can I use color or styling in my resume?

Sparingly. Black and white is safest for ATS. A single color (e.g. for headings) can work ‚Äî but only if it stays text-based, not graphic.

Should I save my resume as DOCX or PDF?

Save as DOCX unless the job posting explicitly allows PDF. Many ATS systems struggle with PDF parsing.

How long should my resume be?

Keep it to one page if you have less than 10 years‚Äô experience. Use two pages only if necessary, and ensure critical info is on the first page.

How can I test if my resume passes ATS screening?

Aside from the Notepad test, use tools like Jobscan or Resume Worded to check ATS compatibility and keyword optimization.
