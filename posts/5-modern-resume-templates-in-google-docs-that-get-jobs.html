How to Use These Templates in Google Docs

  1. Open Google Docs and click Template Gallery → Resumes 8.
  2. Select one of the five templates above and click “Use this template.”
  3. Customize your name, contact, summary, experience, skills, and education.
  4. Enhance with industry keywords, numbers (e.g. “Increased sales by 30%”), and action-oriented bullet points.
  5. Download as PDF (recommended) or share directly via link.

5 Pro Tips for Job-Winning Resumes

  • Stay on one page—if you're early to mid-career, one page is ideal.
  • Prioritize keywords—use exact phrases from job listings in your summary and skill headers.
  • Quantify achievements—numbers get noticed faster.
  • Stick to universal fonts like Arial or Calibri to ensure ATS readability 9.
  • Run an ATS checker on your final PDF to catch formatting problems.
