ATS Safety Tips

  • Avoid tables, headers/footers, images—ATS parses best with plain text.
  • Use standard headings: “Experience,” “Education,” “Skills.”
  • Embed job-specific keywords naturally—don’t keyword-stuff.
  • Run it through free ATS-checkers to catch formatting issues early.

FAQs

1. Which template is best for a creative role?

Spearmint or Modern Writer—creative without compromising readability, and ATS-friendly. Ensure minimal visuals and keep section labels standard.

2. Should I include a photo?

No—for US/Canada. It can confuse ATS. Some European roles or modeling/acting gigs accept photos. 15

3. PDF or .docx?

PDF keeps layout consistent unless a .docx is requested. Docs auto‑save and are easy to share. 16

4. How long should my resume be?

One page for entry to mid‑level; two pages max for senior roles with extensive experience.

5. Can I customize colors and fonts?

Yes! But stay professional: muted tones or subtle accents work best to balance form and function.
