ATS & Readability Tips

  • Simple formatting: Avoid tables, columns, or graphics that confuse ATS.
  • Standard fonts: Arial, Helvetica, and Times New Roman scan best.
  • Keyword alignment: Mirror job listing language (e.g., “project management,” “Python,” “budget forecasting”).
  • Export as PDF: In Google Docs, go to File → Download → PDF for clean formatting.
