Best Practices for Small Store Owners

  • Sequential numbering: Use a pattern like year + vendor initials + number (e.g., 25-VEN-002).
  • Track POs: Keep a master sheet with PO numbers, vendor, status (“Ordered,” “Received,” “Paid”).
  • Include SKUs: Helps suppliers and internal tracking.
  • Set reminders: Follow up 7 days after dispatch if order isn't confirmed.
  • Store archived copies: Use Drive folders named per year or vendor.
